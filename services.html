Our Services

Supply of skilled labour to the mining and construction industry.

Boilermakers

We provide highly skilled boilermakers experienced in metal fabrication, welding, and maintenance of boilers, tanks, and structural steel for mining and construction projects.

Electricians

Qualified electricians delivering safe and efficient electrical installations, maintenance, fault finding, and compliance services for industrial and construction environments.

Auto Electricians

Expert auto electricians specializing in diagnostics, repairs, and installation of electrical systems in heavy-duty vehicles, machinery, and mining equipment.

Heavy Duty Diesel Mechanics

Experienced diesel mechanics providing maintenance, repairs, and overhauls on heavy machinery and equipment to ensure maximum performance and reliability.

Fitters

Skilled fitters specializing in assembling, installing, and maintaining mechanical equipment and machinery in demanding industrial and mining environments.

Riggers

Certified riggers trained in safe lifting, moving, and positioning of heavy loads using cranes and rigging equipment on construction and mining sites.

Millwrights

Our qualified millwrights provide expert installation, maintenance, and repair of industrial machinery, ensuring optimal performance and minimal downtime.

Mobile Thread Repairs

We offer on-site thread repair services, including removal of broken studs, precision tapping, and installation of durable inserts to restore equipment functionality.

Underground & Surface Construction

We deliver reliable construction services for both underground and surface projects, supporting mining and infrastructure development with skilled labour.

Quantity Surveyors

Our quantity surveyors manage project costs, budgeting, and procurement to ensure financial control and efficiency throughout all project phases.

Supervisors & Site Managers (2.9.2)

Experienced supervisors and certified site managers oversee operations, ensuring projects are completed safely, on time, and in compliance with regulations.

Safety Officers

Our safety officers enforce health and safety standards, conduct risk assessments, and promote a safe working environment on all sites.
